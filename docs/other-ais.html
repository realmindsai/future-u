Other AIs to try

All of tonight's prompts work in any of these. Pick one. Make an account. Bookmark it. The tool matters less than the habit of using it.

The list

  1. Claude Anthropic — our favourite. Honest, careful, good at thinking with you. claude.ai
  2. ChatGPT OpenAI — the one everyone knows. Free tier is solid. chat.openai.com
  3. Gemini Google — built into your Google account. Good for research with sources. gemini.google.com
  4. Grok xAI — on X (Twitter). Less filtered, sometimes useful, sometimes not. grok.com
  5. DeepSeek Chinese model. Free, strong at maths and code. Different politics — watch for it. chat.deepseek.com
  6. Kimi Moonshot AI (China). Handles very long documents — paste a whole textbook. kimi.com

The AI of last resort

If you can't sign up for any of those (no email, no phone, blocked by something), this one works without an account. Use it when nothing else will.

No login · No app · Just go
deepai.org/chat
Why have a fallback? Sometimes the main ones are down, or you've hit your free limit, or you're on a school computer that blocks them. The point isn't which AI — it's that you always have one.

How to pick

  • Start with Claude or ChatGPT. Both are free. Both work for everything tonight.
  • Gemini if you live in Gmail and Docs already.
  • DeepSeek or Kimi if you want a free model with no signup fuss (works with email only).
  • deepai.org/chat when you can't get to any of the others.
Same rule everywhere: don't paste private info — no addresses, passwords, Centrelink numbers, medical or immigration details. Different AIs, same risk.
