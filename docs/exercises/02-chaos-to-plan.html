Take-home

02 · Turn chaos into a plan

AGI moment: AI can organise a messy life problem into next actions.

The hook

You have ten things going on. Work, school, family, money, sleep, the thing you keep meaning to start. You don't need a motivational speech. You need someone to lay it out so you can see the next move.

Try this prompt

Here's my situation: I need to find work, finish school, help at home, and I don't know where to start. Turn this into a simple 7-day plan. Assume I have limited money, limited time, and I get overwhelmed. Give me the smallest first step.

Edit the first line to your actual situation. The messier and more honest you are, the better the plan you get back.

Then make it real

Make the plan more realistic for someone who has [transport problems / family responsibilities / anxiety / no laptop at home / siblings to look after / shift work / pick yours].

Why it lands

Adaptability and resilience aren't a poster slogan — they're a skill you can practise. Turning chaos into a plan is one of those skills. AI gives you a sparring partner to do it with.

Don't trust the first plan

The first plan will be too tidy. That's normal. Push back on it.

  • "This won't work because I work 4–10pm Tuesday and Thursday."
  • "My mum needs me at home Saturday morning."
  • "I tried that last month and I gave up after day 2 because…"

Each pushback makes the plan more yours.
